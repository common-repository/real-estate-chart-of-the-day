Real Estate Chart of the Day publishes relevant real estate- and mortgage-related charts to your blog.

== Description ==

Real Estate Chart of the Day is a widget-based plugin that published relevant real estate- and mortgage-related charts to your blog. Data includes, among others:

1. Existing Home Sales
2. Housing Starts
3. Mortgage rate trends
4. New Home Sales
5. Unemployment Rate
6. Pending Home Sales
7. Housing Market Index
8. Weekly Mortgage Rates

Charts are resizable, randomized and current and available in unlimited colors to match every WordPress theme.

== Installation ==

This section describes how to install the plugin and get it working.

1.  Download the plugin to your local hard drive
2.  Use the "Plugins > Upload" menu to install the plugin
3.  Configure the plugin from Setting > Real Estate Charts
4.  Place the chart anywhere on your blog using the Appearance > Widget menu
== Frequently Asked Questions ==

**Can I use these charts in my printed newsletters?**  
    
No, charts are copyrighted and on available for use on limited license. You may only display them on your blog via the plugin.   
    
**Can I use these charts on my website outside of the plugin?**  
    
No, charts are copyrighted and on available for use on limited license. You may only display them on your blog via the plugin.  
    
**Where do the charts come from?**  
    
The charts are produced by Chartforce.com, an real estate and mortgage chart-making service.  
    
**If this plugin is owned by Chartforce.com, why did I download from http://themortgagereports.com?**  
    
The owner of Chartforce.com is the principal author of The Mortgage Reports. Yes, it's a complicated mess.
